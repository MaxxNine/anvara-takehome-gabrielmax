Highest-impact changes for the detail page
1. Expand the value section into a real sales section

Below the title, add structured blocks like:

Why this placement works
Audience fit
What you get
Ideal use cases

Example:

24-hour homepage placement on a startup-focused publication
Best for awareness campaigns, launches, and credibility building
Strong fit for B2B SaaS, devtools, and founder-focused brands

That immediately makes the listing feel more real and targeted.

2. Add trust and process clarity near the booking panel

Right next to CTA, include:

Verified publisher
Response within 1 business day
Secure booking
Cancel before publisher confirmation
One sponsor per slot

This reduces risk perception.

3. Reframe stats into business meaning

Instead of just:

200K monthly reach
45K subscribers

Try:

Reach: 200K monthly readers
Audience: startup and business readers
Pricing: ~$15 CPM equivalent
Booking pace: high-demand monthly slot

Even if some values are estimated, framing matters.

4. Improve CTA copy

“Book This Placement” is okay, but a slightly more specific CTA may work better:

Request booking
Reserve this placement
Start booking request

That matters if there is an approval step and the user is not instantly charged. It lowers commitment anxiety.

5. Add one strong proof block

Examples:

Why brands book this placement
Recent campaign outcomes
Trusted by startup-focused advertisers
Publisher verified by Anvara

Even lightweight proof helps.
